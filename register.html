Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Indian Recipe Finder.

What types of Indian recipes can I find here?
+

You can explore a wide variety of authentic Indian recipes including North Indian classics like Butter Chicken, Biryani, and Paneer Tikka Masala; South Indian dishes like Dosa and Idli; street food favourites like Samosa; festive sweets like Gulab Jamun and Kheer; and vegetarian staples like Dal Makhani and Aloo Gobi.

How do I register and log in?
+

Fill in the registration form on this page with your full name, email address, and a password (minimum 6 characters). Once submitted, you will be redirected to the login page where you can sign in with your email and password to access the full recipe finder.

Is my personal data stored securely?
+

Yes. Your name, email, and password are stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage — they never leave your device and are not transmitted to any external server. This means your account is only accessible on the specific browser and device where you registered.

What can I do after registering?
+

After registration and login you can: search for any Indian dish by name; browse auto-loaded popular Indian recipes; click on recipe cards to view full ingredient lists, step-by-step cooking instructions, and embedded YouTube cooking videos; and use the quick-access cuisine and dish category filters.

Where does the recipe data come from?
+

All recipes are sourced from TheMealDB, a free public recipe database with thousands of dishes from cuisines around the world. When you search for Indian food, we query their API and display matching results with full ingredients, cooking instructions, and YouTube video links.

Do the recipes include cooking videos?
+

Yes! Many recipes include an embedded YouTube cooking video. When available, you will see a "Watch & Cook" section at the bottom of the recipe detail popup where you can follow along with a video tutorial while cooking.

Can I use this app on my phone?
+

Absolutely! Indian Recipe Finder is fully responsive and works beautifully on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Every element — the search bar, recipe grid, modals, FAQ, and footer — is optimised for touch screens and smaller displays.
